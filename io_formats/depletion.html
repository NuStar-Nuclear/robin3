3. Depletion Specification – depletion.xml¶

Parameters of OpenMC depletion integrator are specified in the depletion.xml file. Designed for commandline users who are not familiar with Python API.

3.1. <algorithm> Element¶

The algorithm of depletion.

Default: CECM

3.2. <timesteps> Element¶

Time steps of depletion.

3.3. <timeteps_units> Element¶

Default: MWd/kg

3.4. <power_density> Element¶

The <power_density> element indicate power density of the model that is used for normalizing reaction rate of depletable materials.

Default: 40

3.5. <solver> Element¶

Default: cram48

3.6. <operator> Element¶

The <operator> element indicates the parameters of transport operator used to perform depletion using OpenMC. It has the following attributes:

chain_file

Path to the depletion chain XML file.

normalization_mode

Indicate how tally results should be normalized. energy-deposition computes the total energy deposited in the system and uses the ratio of the power to the energy produced as a normalization factor. fission-q uses the fission Q values from the depletion chain to compute the total energy deposited.

Default: energy-deposition

diff_burnable_mats

Whether to differentiate burnable materials with multiple instances.

Default: true
